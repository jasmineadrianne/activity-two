Lore

Like most SM acts, aespa arrived in the world surrounded by a whole mythology.
Theirs is a cyberpunk sci-fi concept in which the four members, and their four virtual counterparts called aes,
compose a crime-fighting unit trying to take down a villain known as Black Mamba and save the world.

This is the right place if you want to familiarize yourself with aespa's lore and vocabulary.
Take these courses and learn some of the terms related to aespa's worldview to fully understand their unique metaverse concept.

ae
An alter ego where personality came from all the data uploaded every day. ae's are the perfect version of humans, they are created based on how we want people to see us.
naevis
The guide that supports aespa and ae. She is the gate manager who opens the P.O.S. (Port Of Soul) that guides aespa and aes through the different worlds.
MY
A term in Kwangya which means "The Most Precious Friend". MY is also aespa's fandom name. Their fans are the most precious ones for aespa.
FLAT
A virtual world, the place where aes and naevis lives.
KWANGYA
Unprescribed, infinite extent with no rule and form. A virtual world, beyond the FLAT.
P.O.S (Port of Soul)
An energy source that connects the FLAT and the real world.
SYNK
The connection between the human and ae. It has 3 levels: message, voice, and REKALL. The more humans and ae share their thoughts and feelings, the higher SYNK level.
REKALL
The phenomenon of ae coming into the real world and spending time with the human. It is the highest level of SYNK. An ae can be REKALLED when the SYNK level reaches its peak.
